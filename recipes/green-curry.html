Nashville's Hot Chicken

Nashville's Famous Hot Chicken Recipe

This Southern favorite will put a fire in your belly and is hot enough to sear your taste buds.

Give it a try...if you have the following day to spend expelling hot fire out your backside!

Ingredients

  • 1lb Chicken Thighs
  • 1/3 Cup Lard
  • Nashville Hot Spice sachet
  • Dave's Sweet Dill pickle spears
  • Wonderbread

Steps

  1. Season Chicken with half of the Nashville Hot Sachet. Setting remaining satchet aside.
  2. Fry that chicken in your Commercial fry daddy
  3. In a bowl, mix crisco with the remaining spice satchet.
  4. Add the chicken to the bowl and coat uniformly
  5. Place on top of a slice of white bread and spear a pickle with a wooden pick into the chicken
  6. Feel the burn!
